Intro

The materials in this website:
- My personal handwritten solutions to the exercises for two courses: Calculus and Probability & Statistics.
- A recap of Multivariable Calculus by exercises.

I got a lot from junier students and here is what I'd like to share:
- I vividly recall my initial experiences: my limited English listening skills necessitated asking students to repeat their questions, yet they displayed patience with me.
- I was deeply moved when some of them sincerely expressed their gratitude, recognizing my dedication and effort. This affirmed to me that I was engaged in meaningful work.
- It's an unexpected experience for me to still have the opportunity to assist others with my limited gifts in Math and its related fields. This is an unforgettable memory for me, and I would like to share something that I believe is suitable for public viewing, in the hope that my past work can give a hand to your journey in gasping relevent skills.

Please inform me by email with the necessary details:
- If there is anything you deem inappropriate for public viewing, please let me know, and I will promptly remove it.
- If there are any suggestions to help me improve this website, I would greatly cherish them!
